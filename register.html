    Registration

    At least one unique author of each accepted paper must register on or before 15th January 2024 for the papers to be included for publication. The participating delegates should submit the registration form, copyright form, scanned copy of payment receipt and the camera-ready paper by email to ictacme@kmeacollege.ac.in on or before 31st January 2024.

    Registration Fee

    • PG/UG Students - 3000
    • Research Scholar/ Academicians - 4500
    • Industry Delegates - 3500
    • Participants - 1000
    • Foreign PG/UG Students- 100 USD
    • Foreign Research Scholar/ Academicians - 200 USD

    Registration Process

    1. Authors have to first register in Microsoft CMT (with your email id as the username) to submit the abstract.
    2. After creating the account, login to Microsoft CMT. International Conference on Advanced Computing, Communication and Electrical Systems will be visible on the My Conferences tab. If the conference is not visible in the list, search for ICACCES- 2024 in the All-Conferences section. While clicking on the conference name, a submissions page will open for entering the title and abstract of the paper.
    3. Enter the title of the paper and abstract (300 - 1000 words) in the space provided. Also, select the subject area.
    4. Upload the full paper in the space provided in AAPSampleChapter.
    5. Click on Submit and view the submission summary of the paper.
