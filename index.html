    About the Conference

    International Conference on Advanced Computing, Communication and Electrical Systems

    KMEA Engineering College, Edathala will host the First International Conference on Advanced Computing, Communication and Electrical Systems (ICACCES) from January 11-13, 2024. Researchers, engineers, professors, and business executives from all over the world who are interested in cutting edge developments in hot areas in the field computer engineering, communication and electrical are welcome to apply for ICACCES '2024. Researchers with an interest in developing theory, methods, systems, and applications related to current conference subjects are encouraged to submit original works to the ICACCES '2024 conference.
